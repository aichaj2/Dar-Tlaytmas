About Us

Welcome to Our Restaurant

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Agadir, Dar Tlaytmas offers an authentic journey into the richness of Moroccan cuisine. We are dedicated to preserving traditional recipes while adding a refined modern touch to create a memorable dining experience for every guest.

Our restaurant reflects the charm of Moroccan hospitality with a warm and elegant atmosphere inspired by traditional design and contemporary comfort. Each dish is carefully prepared using fresh ingredients, aromatic spices, and a true passion for Moroccan culinary art.
